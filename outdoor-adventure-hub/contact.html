Frequently Asked Questions

How often are trail conditions updated?

We update trail conditions based on community reports and our own verification. Popular trails are updated weekly during hiking season, while less frequented trails may be updated monthly.

Can I contribute my own trail reviews?

Yes! We welcome community contributions. Use the contact form to submit detailed trail reports, including conditions, photos, and any important updates.

Do you offer guided hikes?

Currently, we focus on providing information rather than guided services. However, we may partner with certified guides for special events - check our newsletter for updates.
