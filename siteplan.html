Website Planning Document

Site Name

Outdoor Adventure Hub GMK

This name was chosen because it clearly communicates the purpose of the website - to serve as a central resource for outdoor enthusiasts. The word "Hub" suggests a comprehensive, go-to destination for all things related to outdoor adventures. The GMK logo represents the founder's initials and adds a personal touch to the brand.

Optional domain availability: outdooradventurehub.com

Site Purpose

The Outdoor Adventure Hub website will provide comprehensive resources for outdoor enthusiasts including:

  • Trail guides and hiking recommendations
  • Gear reviews and recommendations
  • Safety tips and preparation checklists
  • Community events and group outing information
  • User-submitted adventure stories and photos

The site aims to be a one-stop resource for both novice and experienced outdoor adventurers looking to explore nature safely and responsibly.

Scenarios

These scenarios represent questions from our target audience that the website content will address:

  1. I'm new to hiking and want to find easy trails near me with good scenery. What equipment do I need to get started?
  2. I'm planning a multi-day backpacking trip in mountainous terrain. Where can I find detailed trail maps, elevation profiles, and current trail conditions?

Color Schema

Secondary Color

Accent Color
#FF7F50
(Coral)

Additional colors:

  • Text Color: #333333 (Dark Gray) - Used for body text
  • Background Color: #F5F5F5 (Light Gray) - Used for page background
  • Light Color: #FFFFFF (White) - Used for content backgrounds

Typography

The selected fonts balance readability with a sense of adventure and exploration:

Headings: Georgia / Times New Roman (Serif)

This serif font family provides a classic, trustworthy feel appropriate for headings and important text elements.

Body Text: Segoe UI / Tahoma / Sans-serif

This sans-serif font family offers excellent readability on screens and creates a clean, modern look for body content.

Usage:

  • All headings (h1-h6) will use the serif font family
  • Body text, navigation, and footer will use the sans-serif font family
  • Special elements or quotes may use alternative fonts for emphasis
  • The GMK logo uses a bold sans-serif font for clear visibility
